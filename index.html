I am an Assistant Professor in the Statistics Department at ENSAE/CREST. I work at the high-dimensional intersection of optimization, statistics, and probability, and am particularly interested in statistical questions in optimal transport. I recently received my PhD from MIT EECS, where I was fortunate to be advised by Phillipe Rigollet and Guy Bresler.
